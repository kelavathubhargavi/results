About Us


SDMSMK-Sri Durga Malleswara Siddhartha Mahila Kalasala established in 1975 and located in Vijayawada. The Sri Durga Malleswara Siddhartha Mahila Kalasala is a premier college in Andhra Pradesh known as excellent educational standards, various educational programs, grand faculty, and various extracurricular activities and modern infrastructure. SDMSMK-Sri Durga Malleswara Siddhartha Mahila Kalasala affiliated by Krishna University the college supports the highest superior standards and best practices in higher education.
